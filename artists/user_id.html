USER_ID is a first-person immersive experience that invites players to explore digital databases of human lives, each one stored under unique user IDs. Through vivid, first-person experiences, players journey into personal, emotional memories—from moments of joy and passion to struggles and mundane daily life—captured in the digital ether.

The game's narrative unfolds in a world where digital memory storage and AI are deeply intertwined. An enigmatic AI, cryptic and omnipresent, leads players through these personal moments while a looming solar storm introduces an element of mystery. Is the storm a natural event or part of the AI's hidden agenda? The game explores themes of the fragility of digital life and the ethical questions surrounding the dehumanization of human existence through data storage.

At its heart, USER_ID is a collaborative and evolving project, blending interactive art, sound, and story. Players not only witness the exploration of the human condition through memory but are also invited to experience the creative process behind the music and art of the game. The soundscapes, crafted with odd time signatures and deconstructed beats, echo the complexity of the digital world being explored, drawing from a wide range of musical genres including IDM, flashcore, and musique concrète. The project is a living, breathing entity where every interaction is part of an ongoing sonic and artistic journey.

The game and music are being developed with tools like Blender for the creation of 3D environments and Godot for the interactive experience. As the project evolves, it remains open to contributions from collaborators, ensuring that every aspect of USER_ID is a shared creative venture.
